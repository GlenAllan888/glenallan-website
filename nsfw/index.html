Résumé

NSFW

I've spent my life trying to understand human behavior and many things about how humans define normative beliefs, boundaries, and build their ideologies and walls. And I've always been fascinated by the ideas and symbols that cause people discomfort. This is a bit of a study over time, in a curated order, of a number of different projects that I've been a part of, or just some art that I did on my own, with imagery that may or may not offend. Each section will have its own context and description, and as well as each image.
